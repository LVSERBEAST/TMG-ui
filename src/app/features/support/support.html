Common Issues & Solutions

Can't log in to my account

Try resetting your password first. If that doesn't work, check if you're using the correct email address. Still stuck? Contact us with the email you think you used to sign up.

A venue is disputing my review

Document your experience with emails, contracts, or photos if possible. We investigate all disputes fairly and protect musician anonymity. Contact us with your evidence and we'll handle it.

App keeps crashing

First, try updating to the latest version. If crashes continue, restart your phone and try again. Still having issues? Email us with your device type and iOS/Android version.

Can't find a venue to review

Use the "Add Venue" feature in the app to create a new venue listing. Make sure to include accurate location and contact information so other musicians can find it.

Someone is harassing me on the platform

We take harassment seriously. Screenshot the messages and report the user immediately. We'll investigate and take appropriate action, including account suspension if necessary.

Response Times

Note: Response times may be longer during weekends and holidays. For urgent safety issues, include "URGENT" in your subject line.
