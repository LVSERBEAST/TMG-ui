About The Money Gigs

Born from Bad Gig Experiences

The Money Gigs was created by musicians who got tired of showing up to venues that didn't pay, treated artists poorly, or had terrible sound systems. We've all been there - the club that "forgot" to mention they don't have a sound engineer, or the bar owner who suddenly can't find the money they promised.

After one too many nightmare gigs, we decided to build something that would help musicians avoid these situations and find the venues that actually treat artists with respect. The Money Gigs is our solution - by musicians, for musicians.

Our Mission

To empower gigging musicians with the information they need to make smart decisions about where they play. No more gambling on unknown venues. No more getting burned by places with bad reputations that other musicians could have warned you about.

Fair Pay

Help musicians know which venues pay what they promise, when they promise it.

Quality Venues

Rate venues on sound, staff treatment, equipment, and overall experience.

Community

Connect with other musicians, share experiences, and build a stronger scene.

Built by Musicians

Our team knows the grind because we live it. We've played dive bars and concert halls, dealt with shady promoters and amazing venue owners. We've had our gear stolen, our pay withheld, and our load-in times changed at the last minute.

But we've also experienced the magic of great venues - places where the staff treats you like family, the sound engineer knows their stuff, and the pay comes exactly when promised. Those venues deserve recognition, and musicians deserve to know where to find them.

"After getting stiffed on payment for the third time in two months, I started keeping a notebook of venue experiences. That notebook became The Money Gigs."

- Sarah, Founder & Lead Developer

Join the Community

The Money Gigs works because musicians share their real experiences. The more musicians who participate, the better the platform becomes for everyone. Whether you're a weekend warrior or a touring professional, your venue reviews help the entire community.
