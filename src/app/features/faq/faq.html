Frequently Asked Questions

Using The Money Gigs App

How do I rate a venue?

After creating an account, search for the venue or add it if it's not listed. Rate based on payment reliability, sound quality, staff treatment, load-in process, and overall experience. Your honest review helps other musicians make informed decisions.

Can venues see who left specific reviews?

No. Reviews are anonymous to venues. Only other musicians can see your username, and only if you choose to make your profile public. We protect musician anonymity to ensure honest feedback.

What if a venue disputes my review?

We investigate all disputes. If you have documentation (contracts, emails, photos), include them with your review. We only remove reviews that violate our community guidelines or contain false information.

How do I find musicians to collaborate with?

Use the "Connect" feature to search for musicians by location, genre, and instrument. You can message other users directly through the app to discuss potential collaborations or fill-in opportunities.

For Venue Owners

How can I improve my venue's rating?

Focus on the fundamentals: pay musicians on time and as agreed, maintain quality sound equipment, treat artists professionally, and communicate clearly about expectations. Good venues with consistent practices naturally receive positive reviews.

Can I respond to reviews of my venue?

Yes, venue owners can create accounts to respond to reviews professionally. We encourage constructive dialogue, but responses that are hostile or threaten musicians will be removed.

How do I claim my venue's profile?

Contact us at venues@themoneygigs.com with proof of ownership or management authority. Verified venues get a badge and can update their information, post upcoming shows, and respond to reviews.

Payment Issues & Safety

What should I do if a venue doesn't pay me?

Document everything - contracts, emails, texts, photos. Report it in the app and consider reaching out to local musician unions or legal aid. While we can't force payment, negative reviews warn other musicians and create accountability.

How do you handle false or malicious reviews?

We verify suspicious reviews and remove those that violate our guidelines. Users who repeatedly post false reviews are banned. We encourage detailed, factual reviews over emotional rants.

Is my personal information safe?

We take privacy seriously. We don't sell user data, and your contact information is never shared with venues. Location data is only used for venue recommendations and is never precise enough to identify your home or exact location.

Technical Support

The app isn't working on my phone. What should I do?

First, make sure you have the latest version. If problems persist, contact us at hello@themoneygigs.com with your device type, OS version, and description of the issue. Include screenshots if possible.

Can I use the app offline?

Basic venue information and your saved venues are cached for offline viewing. However, you'll need an internet connection to post reviews, search for new venues, or use the messaging features.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account in the app, or email us at hello@themoneygigs.com. Your reviews will remain anonymous but will no longer be tied to your account.
