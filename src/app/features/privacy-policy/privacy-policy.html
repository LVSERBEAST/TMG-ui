Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2024

At The Money Gigs, we understand that musicians need to trust us with sensitive information about venues and their experiences. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your email address, username, and basic profile information. We never require your real name - stage names and pseudonyms are perfectly acceptable.

Venue Reviews and Ratings

Your reviews, ratings, and comments about venues. This information is central to our service and helps other musicians make informed decisions.

Location Data

General location information to show you relevant venues in your area. We use city-level location data, never precise GPS coordinates that could identify your home or exact location.

Communication Data

Messages you send through our app, support requests, and correspondence with our team.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve The Money Gigs platform
  • To show you relevant venues and connect you with other musicians
  • To prevent fraud and maintain the quality of our community
  • To communicate with you about your account or our services
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve our features (aggregated data only)

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We never sell your data. We don't sell user information to advertisers, data brokers, or anyone else. Your information stays within The Money Gigs platform.

What We Share

  • • Your public reviews and ratings (anonymous to venues)
  • • Your username and profile (only if you choose to make them public)
  • • Aggregated, non-identifying statistics about platform usage

What We Don't Share

  • • Your email address or contact information
  • • Your precise location or home address
  • • Private messages or personal communications
  • • Individual user data with venues or third parties

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encryption in transit and at rest, secure data centers, and regular security audits.

Musician Anonymity: We understand that musician anonymity is crucial for honest venue reviews. Venues cannot see which specific users left reviews, and we have technical and policy safeguards in place to maintain this anonymity.

Your Rights and Choices

You can:

  • • Update your profile information anytime
  • • Control who can see your profile and reviews
  • • Delete individual reviews or comments
  • • Export your data
  • • Delete your account entirely

We provide:

  • • Clear privacy settings in your account
  • • Easy data export tools
  • • Prompt response to privacy requests
  • • Transparent communication about changes

Questions About Privacy?

We believe privacy should be simple and transparent. If you have questions about this policy or how we handle your data, we're here to help.
